HOME MADE BURGER

Prep time: 15 mins

Yield: 5

Ingredients






Steps

  1. Mix the pepper, salt, dijon, and Worcestershire into the meat, gently.
  2. Form 8 patties (I have a shallow prep bowl I use to get them all the same size and use a scale to keep it even!)
  3. Heat a grill or a large skillet to high. Cook the patties a few minutes each side until done.
  4. Top with fresh basil leaves, a drizzle of olive oil, and red pepper flakes.
  5. Remove, and add to a toasted bun with lots of lettuce, tomatoes, cheese, onions, and pickles (blargh!!) if you must.
  6. Add ketchup, mustard, and mayo as required.
  7. Devour.
