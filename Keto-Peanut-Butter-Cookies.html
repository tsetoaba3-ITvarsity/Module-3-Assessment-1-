KETO PEANUT BUTTER COOKIES

Prep time:1 hours 30 mins

Yield: 22

Ingredients






Steps

  1. In a medium bowl, combine peanut butter, coconut flour, sugar, vanilla, and salt. Stir until smooth.
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk together melted chocolate and coconut oil.
  3. Using a fork, dip peanut butter rounds in chocolate until fully coated then return to baking sheet. Drizzle with more peanut butter then freeze until chocolate sets, about 10 minutes.
